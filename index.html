What We do

We offer pro-bono group counseling, individual counseling, and psycho-educational sessions for community members affected by racial & community injustice.

Who We Are

Ivette Acevedo-Weatherholtz

Ivette Acevedo-Weatherholtz was born in Mexico and immigrated to Washington State with her family when she was 10 years old. Ivette is a provisionally licensed professional counselor in the state of Missouri who specializes in developmental and complex trauma, attachment, and social-emotional learning in youth ages 2-21. She also often facilitates trauma-informed care and attachment trainings to educators and parents. Ivette is a bilingual therapist and has significant experience in providing somatic-based interventions and EMDR therapy to refugee and immigrant youth.

Jon-Pierre Mitchom

Jon-Pierre Mitchom is a Licensed Professional Counselor. He is also currently a school counselor at Parkway Southwest Middle School and has been working in schools and mental health centers since 2003. Jon-Pierre also works as a St. Louis Family Courts Group Facilitator and has a private practice located in South St. Louis City.

Jill Harding

Jill Harding is a Licensed Professional Counselor in the state of Missouri. She currently works as a grief counselor at Heartland Hospice and also runs her private practice called Magnolia Counseling Services which is located in South City. She has been working with counseling clients in a variety of settings for over 5 years.
